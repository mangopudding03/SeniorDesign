This guide is designed to help you understand how to use the application and to best utilize its features.

---

## 1. Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your:
   - Email
   - Password
4. Log in to access your dashboard

---

## 2. Browsing Workouts

1. Navigate to the **Workouts** page.
2. Use the filter panel to select:
   - Chest
   - Back
   - Legs
   - Arms
   - Shoulders
   - Core
3. Click on a workout to view
4. Click **Favorite** to save it to your profile.

---

## 3. Exploring Recipes

1. Go to the **Recipes** page.
2. Filter by:
   - High Protein
   - Vegetarian
   - No Dairy
   - Calorie Deficit
3. Click a recipe to view:
   - Ingredients
   - Nutrition facts
   - Cooking instructions
4. Click **Favorite** to save.

---

## 4. Using the AI Assistant

1. Click on the little icon on the furthest right of the home page.
2. Ask any relavent questions such as:
   - "Best chest workout for beginners?"
   - "High protein vegetarian meal ideas?"
   - "Exercises during menstrual cycle?"

---

## 5. Chat & Community

1. Go to the **Community** page.
2. Add friends.
3. Start a chat.
4. Join discussions and share fitness progress.

---

## 6. Viewing Favorites

1. Go to your **Profile**.
2. Click **Saved Workouts** or **Saved Recipes**.
